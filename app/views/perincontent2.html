There is shift happening in branch networking strategies.
Before, all traffic from the branch offices would be backhauled to HQ and secured by an on-premise firewall. This made sense when the data center was at HQ.
What happens when you need to access cloud applications?
Traffic goes from branch to HQ to the Internet, and back again. This is inefficient.
Fast forward to today, SaaS and cloud has fundamentally changed the way we use and access data
Apps that used to reside in HQ are moving to the cloud, and your branch networking strategies are changing as a result.
Branch router sales will drop more than 60% by 2020 as half of enterprise routers are replaced by SD-WAN.
SaaS and other cloud services are rapidly decentralizing data traffic flows.
